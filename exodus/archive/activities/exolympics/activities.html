Exodus 2021 Olympics

Welcome to the Exodus 2021 Olympic activities. We are excited to announce the kick off of the first ever Exolympic games!

The Exodus Olympics will be a two week event, kicking off Thursday July 22nd with the Opening Ceremonies hosted by Matt (fightincolts). He'll be playing the hits (elsewhere) to get you in a competitive mood, and may even throw some trivia and random prizes into the mix! If you aren't with us elsewhere*, Kelly and Jae will be on the guild board to make sure you still have the opportunity to participate!

Opening Ceremonies will begin at 6 p.m. NST, Thursday July 22nd. We hope to see you there to kick off our next round of activities!
