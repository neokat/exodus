CODING INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to your bingo board! If you've made it this far, you've gotten your bingo board copy & pasted onto a petpage. You're doing great so far! The only other thing you need to know to manage your board is how to mark a square as completed. In the petpage code, take a look at one of your bingo squares, it'll be surrounded by a tag that looks like this:

(div id="notdone")Bingo square text here(/div)

All you need to do to mark a square as completed is remove the not so that your div tag looks like this instead:

(div id="done")Bingo square text here(/div)

And that's it! Once you save your changes it will color the square gold and cross out the text. You are welcome to delete this how-to paragraph from your board if you so choose, or you can keep it up as a reminder. Happy bingo-ing, and remember you can neomail Kat if you have questions or need help!
