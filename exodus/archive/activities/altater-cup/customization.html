Altater Cup Fashion Week ◈


DO YOU HAVE TEAM SPIRIT?!!! DO YOU LOVE TO DRESS UP YOUR PETS?!!!


The Altater Cup fashion parade needs YOUR pets for Fashion Week! You may enter your pets into two different categories, and for each entry you will receive 250 points for your Altater Cup team! (Yes, you can enter both!)

Since this is a fashion parade and not a contest, there will be no voting on winners. As such, you do not need to own any of the items—you're free to just use Dress To Impress for your creations! However, the neopets that you dress up MUST be one of your own pets.

Theme 1: Your Altater Cup Sister Team
The Altater Cup is all about different previous lands coming together to form 4 big teams, and as such, we want you to celebrate your teammates other teams! Example: As a Mystidel Desert - Darigan Citadel team member, Aik can choose to create a Lost Desert or Mystery Island customization! However, a Darigan Citadel customization won't be allowed for this activity.

Theme 2: One of Your Altater Cup RIVALS
We are also all about friendly rivalries here at the Altater Cup. Create a customization for one of your rival teams! The customization can be a gorgeous or outrageous as you would like—it's up to you! Example: As a Meroonia - Roo Island team member, Kat can choose to create a customization for any team EXCEPT Meridell, Roo Island, or Tyrannia.

(If you're wanting to dress up your pet to celebrate your own team, there's an activity for that too! Check out the Team Spirit activity!)

To enter your customizations, neomail Mai (or send via another means) a link to your DTI customization OR an screenshot of your creation!
