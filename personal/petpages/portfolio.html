Welcome

Hi, I'm Kat! I'm a longtime neopetter who came back to the game in May 2020, and it's been so much fun ever since. I love collecting avatars and stamps, dressing my pets in all sorts of seasonal-wear, and running a guild full of some of my best friends. Neo has also really unlocked my creativity this year: I started writing for the Neopian Times and achieved my childhood dream of the NT Star avatar, I explored poetry and short stories for the site spotlights, and I even started watercolor painting my pets and Neopian characters for the Beauty Contest and Art Gallery.
