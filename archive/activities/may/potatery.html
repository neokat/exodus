The Potatery



Welcome to Exodus' Potatery! Run by Amelia, the Potatery works similarly to the Neopian Kadoatery but instead of items the Kads have been Spudified (thank you Christy!!!) and now ask for avatars or trophies! To feed a Spud, send a neomail to Amelia telling her the name of the kad you want to feed as well as proof of the avatar or trophy.

  • FOR AVATARS: set the avatar to your active, or send her a link to your high scores list for game avatars
  • FOR TROPHIES: Amelia will check your userlookup. You do not need to have the same color trophy as pictured in the image!
