Team Jelly World

Welcome to Team Jelly World!
Unfortunately because we are not quite sure whether Jelly World truly exists there are some restrictions to being on the team.

Team Jelly World players can:
Participate in all activities
Earn individual activity points- just like usual
Redeem individual points at any point during the month

Team Jelly World players cannot:
Participate in the Exodus Altater Cup ranks
Receive the Altater Cup placement prizes

So do not fret, there will still be plenty for you to enjoy in the upcoming month and lots to win!

Because Team Jelly World will not be participating in the Altater Cup, the members will not have their Altador Cup games tracked.
