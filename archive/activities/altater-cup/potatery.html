The Potatery ◈


Welcome to Exodus' Potatery: Altater Cup Edition! Run by Amelia, the Potatery works similarly to the Neopian Kadoatery, but instead of items, the Kads have been Spudified and now ask for Altador Cup games!

To feed a Spud, send a neomail to Amelia telling her the name of the kad you want to feed as well as the game(s) you played in order to feed it. Games must be played all in one day and from today forward, meaning you cannot count anything from past days in the AC.

Feeding a spud earns you 250 points for your Altater Cup Team!
You may only feed 1 spud per refresh. (Just like the real Kadoatery!)

The first person to feed a Spudoatie takes the feed and the points. If you are not the first person, the Potatery gods will grant you the grace of 50 points since you played the games.
