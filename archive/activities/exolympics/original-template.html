EXODUS 2021 OLYMPICS


Welcome to the Exodus 2021 Olympic activities. We are excited to announce the kick off of the first Exodus Olympic games.


The Game Plan


The Exodus Olympics will be a two week event, kicking off Thursday July 22nd with the opening ceremonies hosted by Matt. He'll be playing the hits (elsewhere) to get you in a competitive mood and may even throw some trivia and random prizes in the mix! If you aren't with us elsewhere*, Kelly and Jae will be on the guild board to make sure you still have the opportunity to participate! We hope to see you there to kick off our next round of activities!
Opening Ceremonies will start at 6 p.m. NST, Thursday July 22nd.

We will be wrapping everything up with closing ceremonies - we will announce winners and give out prizes!
Closing Ceremonies will start at 4 p.m. NST, Saturday August 7th.
