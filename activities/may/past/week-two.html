Recolor the Guild

As you have definitely noticed by now, the guild is lacking some serious color. Grey Day really got to us, and Baelia the Grey Faerie made the guild match her personality. She told us that we can fix it, though—it's up to us!

The way to fix it is by participating in activities. Every time someone participates in an activity, the Grey Faerie is encouraged and her spirits are lifted. This activity is a team sport—we all win together! The only prize, of course, is bring the vivid colors back to the guild!



HOW IT WORKS

The Grey Faerie's goal is for us to collectively earn 500 Color Points during the month of May.

Well how the heck do you earn color points?!!?

ONE. Participate in weekly activities (5 new activities/week, 1 person's participation = 1 color point)
TWO. Participate in Spotlight Showdown (each spotlight ENTERED = 1 color point)
THREE. Participate in our board activities (1 person's participation = 1 color point)
FOUR. Post on the guild board! (Every 100 posts = 1 color point. Starting post count: 98,700)
FOUR AND A HALF. If we reach 100,000 total messages this month, the Grey Faerie will award an extra 25 color points!

ARE YOU UP FOR THE CHALLENGE?!?!?!


It's not all or nothing, though. As color points are earned, the meter below will fill up with color! As the meter is filled, more and more color will be returned to the guild. You will get to see your progress happen in real time! (About twice a week, but maybe more)

Spotlight Spree

This month our big activity is SPOTLIGHT SPREE! We are on a mission to enter as many site spotlights around neopets as we can to reach our collective goal as a guild: 50 spotlights entered in total, and 20 spotlights won. At the end of the month, we will have our very own Exodus Newspaper to show off everyone's beautiful submissions for the month! (And hopefully, our members will have a lot more trophies and NP prizes from those spotlight wins too!)

HOW IT WORKS

You will earn stars for entering AND getting selected for any of the creative spotlights listed below! Any spotlight you entered during the month of May counts, even if you entered earlier this week before this activity launched. Only one unique submission counts for each guild submission (for instance, you do not get multiple stars from entering the same art piece into the Art Gallery contest over and over—although it will likely help your chances to get picked by TNT if you enter multiple times!) You can only earn 1 submission star for each unique entry that you have.

Eligible creative contests: Art Gallery, User Lookup Spotlight, Neopian Times, Storytelling Contest, Poetry Gallery, and Gallery Spotlight

You must submit your piece to the relevant spotlight AND host your submission on a petpage and neomail it to Kat to earn your star for submission. At the end of the month, we will have an Exodus Newspaper to show off all of our work, whether it was selected for a spotlight or not! (Competition can be tough and it can be hard to get selected by TNT—don't give up!)

Truth or Dare?

Truth or Dare is BACK BABY! We had so much fun with it that we are doing it again.

At any point during the day, Kat or Lynn can hop onto the board and ask the age old question: truth or dare?

Everyone who is around that answers within a ~10 minute window will be able to answer with their choice of truth or dare. You will then be given a truth or dare to complete by either Kat or Lynn. If you complete the task, you win a prize. If you fail the task, well......... we aren't going to talk about what happens if you fail :) Because you won't :) Right? :)

Your truth could be as simple as "what was your favorite neopet when you first started playing?" or as saucy as "tell me your most unpopular opinion about UCs." The dares can be funny, like "set your avatar to I'm Smelly and use it as your default font for 24 hours" or they can be totally involved, requiring you to make silly boards on the AC or post a propaganda message on the board every time someone asks as simple a question as "how are you?" to the group.

Truths and dares and their resulting answers or success/failure status will be posted here for all to see!

The prize for completing a TRUTH will be greyscale and dull, while the prize for completing a DARE will be a lot more colorful!

PRIZES

Successfully completing a truth: Grey Faerie Card (~20k)
Successfully completing a dare: Rainbow Negg Plushie (~45k)



NOTE: you are eligible to complete more than one truth or dare, but you may only have one ACTIVE truth or dare at a time. That means you can't get another truth or dare while you are still completing the first one!
