Danpo Update - December 2021

CURRENT STATUS: THE RESULTS ARE IN. Click here to jump to the results.

PLEASE NOTE THAT THESE RESULTS ARE NOT YET REFLECTED ON DANPO. PAGE UPDATES COMING WITHIN THE WEEK.


~~~

Hi! I’m Kat, and I host Danpo, the community value guide for UC trading.

Danpo is long overdue for an update, and now is the time. It was voted to skip the summer update due to TNT confirming that they would be re-introducing UC creation back to the game. Since that hasn’t happened yet, we will proceed with nominations and a vote to update Danpo. This update will only address pets that need to be moved to different tiers, and will happen in the following way:

Part 1: Nominations, December 15-19
Jump to the nominations section to read more.

Part 2: Voting, December 20-30 A new board will be created to collect votes for all nominated tier moves in an agree/disagree format. A ballot and petpage template will be provided, with more detailed information when the time comes. For a pet to move, it must earn at least 60% “agree” votes.

Results will be announced and recorded on this petpage, with updates to the tier guide taking place in early January.

As a reminder, pet values and tiers are all subjective and voted on by the community. This trading guide is not law. It is merely a guide to help determine general pet values. Additionally, the update process for Danpo is intended to reflect how pets are currently trading. It is NOT meant to reflect how you want pets to be valued.

rules

Updating the guide is a big undertaking with lots of moving parts and communication involved. Please follow these guidelines to make it run smoothly!

You may only make nominations once, from one account. If you are caught abusing nominations, you'll be banned from these votes and suggestions in the future. I'm not trying to falsely inflate or deflate anything.

You may only vote once, from one account. If you are caught abusing the vote, you'll be banned from these votes and suggestions in the future. I'm not trying to falsely inflate or deflate anything.

Please only nominate and vote based on your experience trading on the PC. The tier guide is meant to reflect how pets are currently trading, so only contributing opinions based on the knowledge you have is key.

Brief explanations are encouraged with each vote. A few simple sentence examples: ``I traded X pet in the past and this is reflective of that`` or ``I noticed a few of X pet trading this way before``. You may repeat reasoning if it applies more than once, and you may also be as wordy as you'd like if you have a lot to say. If you have arguments against a certain tier move, those may also be included. Explanations are not necessary for nominations.

Please refrain from posting joke suggestions on the nomination or voting boards.

voting process

The voting process has now ended. I am working on recording ballots, aggregating votes, determining which pets will move, and presenting that information. My current goal is to have this information updated by Monday, January 10. If that changes, I will update this page.

~~~~~

The voting process will run from December 22 to Jan 2. (Delayed a few days from original plan.)

I received 2665 nominations from 125 people. From there, the threshold was increased so that only moves that received 5+ net nominations would appear on the ballot. There are 140 items on the ballot, including both tier moves and border moves.

All ballot items that receive at least 60% yes votes will be changes that are taken into effect on Danpo. This 60% will be calculated out of total votes received for a ballot item. (For instance, if only 10 people vote on a given item, at least 6 people need to vote "yes" for the change to take place. If 100 people vote for a given item, 60 people would need to vote "yes". The percentage will be calculated from the total number of votes for each item, not from the total number of respondents, because you are encouraged to skip voting for pets/tiers that you do not have experience trading in.)

Voting Instructions

Please read instructions before voting!

Please vote ONLY on pets/tiers that you are familiar with, and remember that your vote is intended to reflect how pets are currently trading.

You will vote by REMOVING the yes/no option you are NOT selecting, leaving only the option you ARE selecting. For example, if I wanted to vote on "Robot Blumaroo +25" (bogus example/in reverence of Danpo), the line on my ballot would look like this originally:
robot blumaroo +25: yes / no / reasoning: ___________

If I wanted to vote to vote YES to this change, the line would look like this:
robot blumaroo +25: yes / I have seen robot blumaroos trading way above their tier

However, if I were to vote NO to this change, the line would look like this:
robot blumaroo +25: no / I only see robot blumaroos trading within their current tier

Brief explanations are encouraged with each vote. A few simple examples: ''I traded X pet in the past and this is reflective of that'' or ''I noticed a few of X pet trading this way before''. You may repeat reasoning if it applies more than once, or you may also be as wordy as you'd like if you have a lot to say.

If you wish to refrain from voting on a ballot item, you can remove that line entirely.

The Ballot

All ballots must be submitted on a petpage, and the link must be neomailed to superkathiee. (If you really, really need to neomail your votes, or you are only voting on a handful of pets, you can do so by copying the ballot text from here, filling it out, and neomailing superkathiee. Please limit that to 2 neomails MAXIMUM. This means that if you are neomail voting, you need to select the items you wish to vote for very carefully.)

The template for the ballot is in the text box below. Simply copy the text from the box and paste it into a pet page, and then edit each line to reflect your vote. Click here to see what the ballot template looks like on a petpage. The instructions above are also provided on the template in case you need to refer back to them.

QUICKLINKS: edit a petpage | neomail Kat

Please leave petpage ballots up until January 5, 2022.

nomination process

The nomination process has now ended. It's time to vote, click here to jump to voting!

~~~~~

It is preferred that nominations are posted on the board to help me keep everything in one place. However, you may neomail your nominations to superkathiee if you wish, and if you do, please post on the board "I am neomailing my nominations to superkathiee" so that I can ensure I received your neomail and it did not get lost in the spaghetti code of neopets dot com.

Nominations can be submitted for both tier moves and border color addition/removal. The border meanings are as follows:

Popular Pets

These pets are widely popular and typically trade well.
Rare Pets

These pets are generally more difficult to find.
Common Pets

In an average day, you'll see a few of these pets up for trade.


Please submit nominations in the following format, using the Robot Blumaroo as an example.

For tier increase or border color addition:
Robot Blumaroo +1
Robot Blumaroo +Common
Robot Blumaroo +Popular
Robot Blumaroo +Rare

For tier decrease or border color removal:
Robot Blumaroo -1
Robot Blumaroo -Common
Robot Blumaroo -Popular
Robot Blumaroo -Rare

Please place one suggestion per line and please fully spell out all color and pet names (e.g. Royalgirl, Royalboy, Plushie, Cybunny, etc.) It makes my life easier for data collection!

All pets that receive at least 3 net nominations will appear on the ballot for both tier moves and border colors. (A +1 and a -1 for the same pet will cancel each other out, so a “net nomination” means at least 3 nominations in a specific direction for one pet without those noms being cancelled.)

For tiers where a cancellation isn't possible, at least 5 nominations are needed to appear on the ballot (e.g. there is nothing to cancel a "Tyrannian Quiggle +1" since it's in T1 and cannot go below).

Nominations for a tier jump of 2 or more will be dealt with on a case-by-case basis once all nominations have been received, and will need a minimum of 5 nominations to be considered. If a 2+ tier jump makes it onto the ballot, the process will be specifically explained once voting commences.
