For December, most of the Exodus activities last all month long! A few of them have early sign up deadlines, or a Part 1/Part 2 with a deadline in the middle of the month, and some others you might want to do sooner than others to enjoy the effects within the guild.

We've also brought back the points system this month! That means that the top 3 winners will have a chance to redeem prizes from the winners prize pot, and everyone else can redeem their points for prizes from the shop at the end of the event. Prize redemption will last from January 1st through the 5th! Prizes for the winner pot and the shop will be announced on their respective pages soon.

Points Information

Over on the right, you'll see an updated list of points for all of our members. These are collected through the entire month of December – the competition will end on December 31st.

Points are updated 1-2 times a week. Please be patient in between updates. Multiple members are hosting activities, so by updating 1-2 times a week, that allows the time to collect an accurate count.

There are a few different ways to earn points!

The best way you'll be able to rack up your points is by participating in activities. We have three categories of activities: monthly, weekly, and board and they all offer different amounts of points based on completion!

We also award points to members who have gone above and beyond. Did someone make you a lookup, a font, or perhaps helped you code a page? Did someone grant one of your big NC or NP wishes? Did someone help you get your dream pet? Please let Kat know! You will receive 10 points for being the one to reach out, and they will receive points for doing a good deed. The number of points will be determined depending on the deed.

Prize Pot

There will be three winners on December 31st. Instead of delegating certain rewards for each place, we have created a prize pot.

First place will be allowed to choose two prizes from the pot: one non-item, and one NP item. You will get first choice. You will also receive a bonus mystery gift from council.

Second place will be allowed to choose two prizes from the remaining prize pot, after first place has chosen theirs: one non-item, and one NP item. You will also receive a bonus mystery gift from council.

Third place will be allowed to choose one prize from the remaining prize pot, after first place and second place have both chosen theirs. You will also receive a bonus mystery gift from council.

Boxing Day Sales


This month, check out the guild shop for some holiday deals! We’ll be hosting flash sales throughout the month. You never know what Potatoclaus might have in the sack!

Guild shop sales will occur randomly and be posted on the guild chat when they are taking place, so keep an eye out! There are two types of sales that could occur:

Neopoint Drives will be events where the shop is filled to the brim with items priced at various amounts. The goal is for everyone to buy out the shop. Prior to the sale, one specific item will be earmarked as The Golden Ticket. The person who buys The Golden Ticket will receive a NP item worth 50k or more! All funds raised will go towards the guild, and everyone who participates in this sale will receive points for their donations!

Flash 1k NP Sales are sales where a lot of items, some valuable, some not as much, will be set in the guild shop. EVERYTHING will be priced at 1k. A time will be announced prior to the sale, and when the items drop, it's up to you to find and buy the good ones! No points will be awarded for this activity; the prize is the item(s) you are able to pick up!
